Science-fueled fun

Our motivated team of local entrepreneurs, artists, engineers and makers are providing enriching programs, and building interactive exhibits. The Hawai'i Keiki Musuem uses career-connected, hands-on learning techniques to connect our keiki to the natural world. Our exhibits are thoughtfully designed to attract girls to STEAM by focusing on open-ended interactions and encouraging cooperative play.

On this island, science is literally “all around us”. There are many opportunities for our keiki to become leaders in STEM industries - right here. It is critical that we show our keiki that opportunities here on the Big Island not only exist, but that they are exciting.

Hawai'i Keiki Museum

Keiki is the Hawaiian word for children. We are creating a pathway, helping to guide our keiki on their personal journey toward rewarding careers in STEM industries that create a more sustainable future for us all.

Our Mission it to inspire curiosity, creativity, and innovation by introducing children to science during their early years.

Our exhibits are thoughtfully designed to attract girls to STEM industries. Families who have children with disabilities will feel welcome and included everywhere within the facility.
